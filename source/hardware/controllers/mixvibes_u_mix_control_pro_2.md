# MixVibes U-Mix Control Pro 2

:::{versionadded} 1.11
:::

-  [Manufacturer's product page](http://www.mixvibes.com/content/products/u-mix-control-pro-2-pictures) (archived)
-  [Forum thread](http://mixxx.org/forums/viewtopic.php?f=7&t=4892)

The U-Mix Control Pro 2 is a MIDI DJ controller with an internal
sound card and two inputs for turntables or any audio source with RCA.
It offers a changeable crossfader, adjustable crossfader curve and
individual touch level sensors.

The U-Mix Control Pro differs only in cue button labels. The U-Mix
Control 2 (without "Pro", no internal sound card) is also compatible
with this mapping.

## Features

- Crossfader, volume faders and pitch bend sliders
- Master, gain and EQ knobs with kill switches
- Cue/master mix (headphone) control
- Cue, play, sync, pitch bend, hotcue, headphone buttons
- Load A/B buttons
- Jog wheels with scratch and jog modes
- Media knob for library browsing
- LED indicators for clipping, EQ kill, cue/play/BPM/sync, hotcues,
  headphone, load status, jog (keylock), and bend

## Special functions

- **BPM tap**: Press :hwlabel:`SHIFT` + :hwlabel:`SYNC`
- **Keylock**: Press :hwlabel:`JOG`

## Known limitations

- Sample deck management is not available
- Loop knob and loop setting are not available
- FX section is not yet mapped
- MIC level control is a hardware function (mic is hardwired through master output)
- Headphone level control is independent from Mixxx
- Touch level sensitivity is hardware only
